      </p>
    </section>

    <section className="notice-card">
      <h2>AI Policy for Contributing Projects</h2>
      <p>
        Before contributing, review the project or repository AI policy. Some communities require disclosure of AI use, while others limit or prohibit AI-generated code. Follow contribution rules and be transparent about AI assistance.
      </p>
    </section>

    <section className="notice-card">
      <h2>Environmental Impact and Cost</h2>
      <p>
        Consider the environmental and financial cost of AI-assisted coding. Large models use substantial compute, energy, and money. Avoid unnecessary API calls and use AI intentionally with efficient workflows.
      </p>
    </section>
